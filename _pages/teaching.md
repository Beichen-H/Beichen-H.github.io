I serve as a Course Assistant (CA) for **[STAT 207: Data Science Exploration](https://exploration.stat.illinois.edu)** at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

This core course explores the complete data science pipeline, bridging theoretical statistical concepts with hands-on computational applications. 

#### Course Assistant Responsibilities
*(August 2025 – Present)*

- **Technical Mentorship:** Guide students through the implementation of data science workflows using **Python (Pandas, Scikit-learn)**, Jupyter Notebooks, and **Git/GitHub** version control.
- **Lab & Office Hours:** Facilitate weekly open lab sessions, helping students troubleshoot complex programming bugs and master concepts in data wrangling, visualization, and statistical modeling.
- **Academic Evaluation:** Evaluate individual and group lab assignments, providing constructive feedback on code efficiency, statistical inference, and machine learning model assumptions (e.g., multiple linear/logistic regression, classification).

For more details on the curriculum, assignments, and course structure, please visit the official site:
